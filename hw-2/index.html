The World of Trigonometry

Trigonometry is the foundation for architecture, engineering, and even the physics of video games. Let's break down the essentials.

The Core Functions

The three primary functions are used to find unknown distances in a triangle:

  • Sine (sin): Opposite / Hypotenuse
  • Cosine (cos): Adjacent / Hypotenuse
  • Tangent (tan): Opposite / Adjacent

Common Applications

  1. Measuring building heights.
  2. Navigating planes and ships.
  3. Creating 3D CGI for movies.
